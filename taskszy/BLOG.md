## Blog Post Structure

### Title (H1)
**Format**: [Number] [Adjective] [Keyword] [for/in] [Target Audience] [Year]

**Examples**:
- "15 Best Task Management Software for Agencies in 2026"
- "How to Choose Project Management Platform: Complete Guide"
- "Task Management vs Project Management: Key Differences Explained"

**Rules**:
- Include primary keyword
- 50-60 characters
- Compelling/clickable
- Year (if relevant)

---

### Meta Description
**Character count**: 155-160 characters

**Template**:
"[Value proposition]. [Key benefit]. [Secondary benefit]. [CTA]. [Trust signal]."

**Example**:
"Discover the 15 best task management software for agencies. Compare features, pricing & reviews. Get expert recommendations to boost team productivity. Updated 2026."

---

### Introduction (100-150 words)

**Paragraph 1** (Hook):
Start with a problem, question, or surprising statistic.

Example:
"Are you spending more time managing tasks than actually completing them? You're not alone. 67% of projects fail due to poor task management, according to PMI research."

**Paragraph 2** (Context):
Explain why this topic matters.

Example:
"Choosing the right task management software can make or break your team's productivity. With over 200+ tools on the market, finding the perfect fit is overwhelming."

**Paragraph 3** (Preview):
Tell readers what they'll learn.

Example:
"In this comprehensive guide, we'll compare the top 15 task management tools, break down their features, pricing, and help you choose the best one for your agency."

**Include**:
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Internal link to related content
- Clear value proposition
